Sonar

  • Sonar is a technique based on the principle of reflection of ultrasonic sound waves.
  • These waves propagate through water and reflect on hitting the ocean bed or any object obstructing its path.
  • Sonar has been widely used in submarine navigation, communication with or detection of objects on or under the water surface hazard identification, etc.
  • There are two types of sonar technology used — passive and active.
