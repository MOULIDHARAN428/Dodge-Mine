How to avoid marine mines while still getting to your destination.

By observing the differences, you can ensure that sonar inputs are being received appropriately.

  • When you first visit the portal, you will see facts, instructions, and a prediction choice.
  • Simply select the prediction option and follow the instructions outlined below.
  • Take the sonar inputs and place them in the sonar input boxes provided.
  • On your next move, the trained ML model will compute and show you whether a marine mine or rock is there.
  • This allows you to dodge marine mines and arrive at your destination safely.
